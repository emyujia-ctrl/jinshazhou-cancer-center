Our Technology

World-Leading Radiotherapy Equipment

Our center has invested in the most advanced radiotherapy systems available globally, ensuring precision, safety, and optimal treatment outcomes for every patient.

CyberKnife M6

Accuray CyberKnife M6

The 6th generation AI-powered robotic radiosurgery system with real-time tumor tracking and multi-leaf collimator. Treats tumors anywhere in the body with sub-millimeter precision in 1-5 sessions.

Robotic Radiosurgery Real-Time Tracking Brain & Body Tumors

TomoTherapy

Accuray Helical TomoTherapy

Integrates IMRT, IGRT, and DGRT into one system. 360-degree helical delivery with precision targeting accuracy of ±0.1mm, ideal for complex and multiple tumors.

360° Helical Delivery ±0.1mm Precision Complex Tumors

TrueBeam

Varian TrueBeam

High-precision image-guided linear accelerator with HyperArc technology. Delivers rapid, accurate treatment for a wide range of cancers including lung, liver, prostate, and brain.

Image-Guided HyperArc Versatile Application

HyperArc

Intracranial SRS Technology

Non-coplanar stereotactic radiosurgery technology specifically for intracranial tumors. Sub-millimeter accuracy treating multiple brain metastases simultaneously in 10 minutes.

Brain Metastases Non-Coplanar 10-Min Treatment

Halcyon

Varian Halcyon System

The world's first medical accelerator with 100% image guidance. Full digitalization, AI-powered, 6-minute complete treatment sessions with high patient comfort and quiet operation.

100% Image Guidance 6-Min Sessions AI-Powered

Ethos

AI Adaptive Radiotherapy

The world's first online adaptive radiotherapy platform. AI real-time adaptation to daily anatomical changes. Personalized treatment every session for prostate, bladder, cervical, and rectal cancers.

AI Adaptive Daily Adaptation Pelvic Cancers

Brachytherapy

GammaMedplus IX HDR

High Dose Rate afterloader for precise internal radiation. Delivers radioactive sources directly to tumor sites. Short treatment times, outpatient procedures, high safety with minimal side effects.

Internal Radiation High Dose Rate Cervical & Prostate

MRI + CT Simulation

4D MRI & Dual-Energy CT

Siemens 3.0T MRI with motion perception, 4D MRI respiratory gating, and dual-energy large-bore CT. Real-time adaptive imaging for precise tumor tracking during treatment.

4D MRI Motion Tracking Real-Time
Team Approach

Multidisciplinary Team (MDT) Model

Our MDT brings together experts from multiple disciplines to create the most effective, personalized treatment plan for every patient.

  • Radiation Oncology

    Expert radiation oncologists using cutting-edge technology including CyberKnife, TrueBeam, and TomoTherapy for precise tumor targeting.

  • Medical Oncology

    Chemotherapy, immunotherapy, and targeted therapy specialists providing systemic treatment solutions.

  • Surgical Oncology

    Experienced surgeons using minimally invasive and robotic techniques for tumor removal when surgery is indicated.

  • Traditional Chinese Medicine

    TCM integration including acupuncture, herbal medicine, and rehabilitation to reduce side effects and improve recovery.

  • Interventional Therapy

    Minimally invasive procedures including TACE, radiofrequency ablation, and particle implantation for localized treatment.
